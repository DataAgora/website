Intro

DataShark is a decentralized data marketplace where data providers (users) meet data buyers (companies), without the need for a traditional data broker. We align incentives between data providers and buyers with a transactional blockchain layer.

Users are remunerated for sharing access to data they generate and are already providing for free. Users can give us access to their entertainment history, location data, and social media graphs, and we can pay them $10/month as passive income. If you would be interested in participating in our beta, please fill out this survey.

Companies have a rich, consistent and high quality source of data of any type for product development or research. We assure quality and prevent fraud with auditable machine learning techniques. If you would be interested in acquiring high-quality data in our beta, please contact us.
